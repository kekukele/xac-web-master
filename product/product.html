ECR POS & Customer Facing Device

ECR POS, also known as a point of sale terminal or EFTPOS terminal, is a device which interfaces with payment cards to make electronic funds transfers and operated by store clerks. A customer facing device is a device on countertop which is operated by clients; therefore, friendly client’s payment interface and secured customer’s privacy protection are critical. With XAC's Secure Android Platform, sensitive data are protected under world’s latest security standard.
